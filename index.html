Time and again ,it is proved how basic education can set the foundation of one’s life.Yet in India, the education sector is marred with several issues- technological divide,shortage of teachers,lack of basic classroom resources,increasing school dropout rates and so on.These issues can viewed in terms of the rural urban divide,gender disparity,social and economic backwardness and so on.These gaps in the society has been exacerbated thanks to the COVID lockdown.

We ,at Vidya Bandhan ,aim to ensure that the education which the underprivileged students from NGOs and schools receive is uninterrupted,qualitative and improvised through qualitative lectures in the form of videos.

Our team consisting of talented educators,researchers and video editors are here to actively engage in making education for all a reality!

Our aim is to act as mobilisers of good spirited educators from among the youth to teach the students of your organisation in accordance to the requirement of the age group of the students and syllabus which the they must cover.
